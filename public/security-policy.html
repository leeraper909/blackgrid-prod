Security Policy

We appreciate responsible security research. If you believe you’ve found a vulnerability, please report it to us.

Contact

  • Email: security@example.com
  • Preferred-Languages: en

Disclosure & Safe Harbor

If you make a good-faith effort to comply with this policy during your research, we will consider it authorized, will not initiate legal action, and will work with you to understand and resolve the issue quickly.

In Scope

  • Main site: https://blackgrid-prod.netlify.app/
  • Serverless endpoints under: /api/*

Out of Scope

  • Denial of service (DoS), brute force, or volumetric attacks
  • Automated scraping or data exfiltration from /trial/* (demo area)
  • Third-party services and assets outside our control

Rate Limits

API calls are rate limited (see x-ratelimit-* headers). Please keep tests within these limits.

Report Format

  • Issue summary and impact
  • Step-by-step reproduction (URLs, payloads, screenshots)
  • Suggested remediation if known

Thank you for helping keep users safe.
